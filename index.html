Claim Journey

  1. Start Claim
  2. Claim Submitted
  3. Under Review (for complex cases)
  4. Approved or Rejected

Simple claims can be auto-approved or auto-rejected based on rules. Complex claims are routed to human review.

Documents Required

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Policy number or policy document
  • Incident summary and date
  • Supporting evidence (photos, invoices, reports)
  • Bank details (if reimbursement applies)
